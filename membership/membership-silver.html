How to Become a Member

Becoming a member of the Albay Chamber is simple. Just fill out our member registration form and Chamber staff will follow up with you. Our memberships run on a calendar year, January through December, so depending on the month you join, you will pay a prorated rate. Our staff will discuss the options with you. You also have an option to pay a monthly or quarterly amount on autopay to your credit card. This is an easier approach to paying on an ongoing basis. Our staff will review this option with you.
